## What This Result Does Not Prove

This does not prove real-world BCI reliability, medical usefulness, diagnosis ability, treatment ability, emotion detection ability, or performance on people outside the dataset.

## Leakage And Limitations

- Subject-aware testing is stronger than random trial-level splitting across subjects.
- EEG datasets can contain subject, session, hardware, and preprocessing artifacts.
- A high score on one dataset does not imply a deployable BCI system.
- This project is not medical software.

## Notes And Warnings

- Raw files expose 26 channels; this card lists 22 EEG channels used for motor-imagery analysis.
- Metrics are dataset-specific and do not prove real-world BCI reliability.
- Avoid trial-level random splits across subjects; subject-aware splits reduce leakage risk.
- This is not medical software and should not be used for diagnosis or treatment.
